Welcome to Oracle Siebel CRM Training

Welcome to your comprehensive journey into Oracle Siebel CRM! This training provides the foundation you need to understand and work with one of the world's most powerful customer relationship management platforms.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand CRM concepts and business value
  • Explore Siebel CRM's key features and capabilities
  • Learn about Siebel's modular architecture
  • Master configuration using Siebel Tools
  • Understand data architecture, workflows, and customization
  • Work with views, applets, and user roles
  • Manage customer data, service requests, and analytics
  • Build practical skills through hands-on labs

This training is designed for both business users and technical professionals. No prior CRM experience is required.
