Customer Relationship Management (CRM) is a business strategy and technology platform to manage interactions with customers and prospects.

Two Dimensions of CRM

1. Business Strategy

CRM is your company's "memory" ‚Äî remembering every customer, conversation, purchase, and problem to build profitable relationships.

  • Customer Focus: Build relationships, not just transactions
  • Process Optimization: Streamline sales, marketing, and service
  • Data-Driven Decisions: Use insights to target, upsell, and retain

2. Technology Platform

Siebel CRM provides tools to execute the strategy:

  • Centralizes Data: Single source of truth
  • Automates Processes: Lead assignment, follow-ups, workflows
  • Enables Collaboration: Teams see the same info
  • Provides Insights: Analytics and reports on customer behavior
