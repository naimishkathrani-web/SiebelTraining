Telecom Context: How Modules Work Together (Telecom Operator)

  • Marketing targets segments (e.g., rural prepaid data users) ‚Üí leads flow to Sales for high-value prospects
  • Sales converts opportunities to Orders using product catalog and pricing
  • Order Management integrates with Provisioning to activate services/SIMs
  • Service handles SRs for coverage, billing queries, SIM swap, etc., with SLA-driven workflows
  • Analytics closes the loop with churn detection, ARPU insights, and offer performance

Why This Matters (Telecom Operator)

  • Consistent hand-offs reduce dropped steps across Marketing ‚Üí Sales ‚Üí Service
  • Provisioning and Billing integrations ensure orders are fulfilled and charged correctly
  • SLA-driven SRs improve NPS for network-related complaints
  • Next-best-action boosts retention for high-risk prepaid segments
