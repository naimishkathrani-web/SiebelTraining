Segmentation divides customers into groups with similar characteristics for targeted marketing and service.

Segmentation Strategies

Demographic Segmentation

Age, gender, income, location

Telecom Example: Youth (18-25) for data plans; Seniors (60+) for simple voice plans

Behavioral Segmentation

Usage patterns, purchase history, engagement level

Telecom Example: High data users (>10GB/month); Low ARPU customers

Churn Risk Segmentation

Likelihood to leave based on complaints, contract end date, competitor activity

Telecom Example: High churn risk ‚Üí Retention offers
