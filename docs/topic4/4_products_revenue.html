Products & Revenue Management

Product Catalog Structure

Siebel's product catalog supports complex hierarchies, bundles, and configurable offerings for telecom services.

Product Types

  • Service Plans: Unlimited 5G, Shared Data, Prepaid
  • Devices: Smartphones, tablets, wearables
  • Add-ons: International roaming, hotspot, insurance
  • Bundles: Device + Plan + Accessories (one-click activation)

Pricing & Discounts

Price Lists

  • Standard Pricing: MSRP for all products
  • Volume Discounts: 10+ lines = 10% off, 100+ lines = 20% off
  • Promotional Pricing: Limited-time offers (e.g., "Free device with 2-year contract")
  • Contract Pricing: Enterprise agreements with negotiated rates

Discount Approvals

Approval Workflow:
  • 0-10% discount: Auto-approved
  • 11-20% discount: Manager approval required
  • 21%+ discount: VP Sales approval required

Revenue Recognition

Revenue Types

  • One-Time Revenue: Device sales, activation fees
  • Recurring Revenue: Monthly plan charges (MRR)
  • Usage Revenue: Overage charges, international calls

Calculation Example

  • Opportunity: 50 users, $80/month unlimited plan
  • One-Time: 50× $800 devices = $40,000
  • Monthly Recurring: 50× $80 = $4,000/month
  • Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR): $4,000× 12 = $48,000
  • Total Contract Value (TCV): $40,000 + (2 years× $48,000) = $136,000

Product Bundles

Pre-configured packages for common use cases:

  • Executive Bundle: iPhone 15 Pro + Unlimited Premium + AppleCare
  • Field Worker Bundle: Rugged device + Unlimited Data + GPS tracking
  • Family Bundle: 4 lines shared data + streaming services

Customization & Add-ons

Cross-Sell Opportunities:
  • Customer selects base plan → System suggests add-ons (roaming, insurance)
  • Rule-based recommendations: "Customers with iPhone often add AppleCare"
  • Upsell prompts: "Upgrade to Unlimited for only $10 more"
