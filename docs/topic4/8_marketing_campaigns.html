Marketing Campaigns

Campaign Overview

Siebel Marketing enables multi-channel campaigns with segmentation, execution, and ROI tracking. Campaigns drive lead generation, customer retention, and upsell opportunities.

Campaign Types

1. Awareness Campaigns

  • Goal: Brand visibility, product launch announcements
  • Channels: Email, social media, webinars
  • Example: "Introducing 5G Home Internet" email blast to 50K subscribers

2. Lead Generation Campaigns

  • Goal: Capture new prospects
  • Channels: Trade shows, web forms, paid ads
  • Example: "Request a 5G Demo" landing page with form

3. Nurture Campaigns

  • Goal: Move leads through sales funnel
  • Channels: Drip emails, retargeting ads
  • Example: 6-week email series educating prospects on 5G benefits

4. Retention/Upsell Campaigns

  • Goal: Prevent churn, cross-sell/upsell
  • Channels: SMS, email, in-app messages
  • Example: "Upgrade to Unlimited for $10/month" to existing customers

Campaign Workflow

6-Step Process:
  1. Define Audience: Segment customers (e.g., "Customers with data overage charges")
  2. Create Content: Email copy, landing pages, call scripts
  3. Schedule Execution: Send emails on Tuesday 10 AM
  4. Execute Campaign: System sends 10K emails
  5. Track Responses: Open rate, click rate, conversions
  6. Measure ROI: Revenue generated vs. campaign cost

Multi-Channel Campaigns

  • Email: Primary channel, personalized messaging
  • SMS: High open rate (98%), time-sensitive offers
  • Social Media: Facebook/LinkedIn ads, organic posts
  • Direct Mail: Physical brochures for high-value prospects
  • Telemarketing: Follow-up calls to engaged leads

Campaign Metrics

  • Reach: 10,000 emails sent
  • Open Rate: 2,500 opened (25%)
  • Click-Through Rate: 500 clicked (5% of sent, 20% of opened)
  • Conversion Rate: 50 purchased (1% of sent, 10% of clicked)
  • Revenue: 50× $100/month plan = $5,000 MRR ($60K annual)
  • Campaign Cost: $2,000 (creative + email platform)
  • ROI: ($60K - $2K) / $2K = 2,900% ROI

Example: 5G Upgrade Campaign

Campaign Details:
  • Target Segment: Customers on 4G plans with high data usage (5,000 customers)
  • Message: "Your 4G plan can't keep up. Upgrade to 5G for 2× speed, same price!"
  • Offer: Free 5G-enabled device with 2-year contract
  • Channels: Email + SMS + In-app notification
  • Results: 300 upgrades (6% conversion), $90K MRR increase
