Introduction to Reporting & Analytics

Why Reporting Matters

Data without insights is just noise. Siebel's reporting capabilities transform raw CRM data into actionable intelligence for sales forecasting, service optimization, marketing ROI, and executive decision-making.

What You'll Learn

Reporting Fundamentals

  • Standard Reports: 100+ pre-built reports for common use cases
  • Custom Reports: Build your own using Report Designer
  • Advanced Features: Calculated fields, joins, sub-queries

Business Intelligence

  • Dashboards: Interactive visual analytics with drill-down
  • KPIs: Track performance against targets
  • Real-Time Analytics: Live data updates and trending

Enterprise Integration

  • BI Publisher: Enterprise-grade reporting with Oracle BI
  • Data Export: Excel, PDF, CSV for external analysis
  • Scheduled Delivery: Automated email distribution

Reporting Use Cases

TelecomCo Scenarios:
  • Sales VP: "Show me pipeline by rep, win rate trends, and Q4 forecast accuracy"
  • Service Manager: "Report on SLA compliance, average resolution time, and customer satisfaction scores"
  • Marketing Director: "Campaign ROI, lead conversion rates, and cost per acquisition"
  • CFO: "Monthly recurring revenue, churn rate, and customer lifetime value"

Report Types

  • Tabular Reports: Row/column format (like Excel spreadsheet)
  • Summary Reports: Grouped data with subtotals (e.g., revenue by region)
  • Chart Reports: Visual graphs (bar, line, pie)
  • Dashboard Widgets: KPI tiles, gauges, trend lines
  • Ad-Hoc Queries: On-the-fly analysis without pre-built reports
